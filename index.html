Location

You will find us on the West-Coast of Meganisi, directly opposite of the Poros Mountain, set in a beautiful green garden with plenty of shade beneath the olive trees.


Enjoy a warm meal by the sea, with great views of the blue sea, the south of lefkada, Kefalonia and Ithaca.

Gaze upon the many boats passing through the Meganisi channel and enjoy the nearly constant natural breeze the location provides at noon.
